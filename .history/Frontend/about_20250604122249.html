About GreenEart

Committed to delivering environmental solutions that build a sustainable future for all.

Who We Are

GreenEarth Environmental Consultancy is a multidisciplinary environmental firm based in Kenya, dedicated to helping businesses and organizations comply with environmental regulations while promoting sustainable practices.

Our team comprises experienced environmental scientists, auditors, and sustainability experts who bring a wealth of knowledge to projects ranging from Environmental Impact Assessments (EIAs) to ISO certification support.

Our Mission

To empower communities, industries, and governments with expert environmental consultancy that fosters long-term ecological balance and responsible development.

Our Vision

A future where sustainable practices are embedded in every sector of the economy, ensuring a healthy planet for future generations.
