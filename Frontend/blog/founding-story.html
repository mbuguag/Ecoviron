Our Founding Story (2015)

Posted on Jan 10, 2024 by GreenPath Team

GreenPath Consulting was established in 2015 with a mission to help organizations navigate the growing environmental compliance requirements in Kenya. What started as a two-person initiative quickly grew into a trusted consultancy...

Our core values were (and still are): sustainability, community impact, and compliance with both national and global environmental regulations.

Today, we are proud to have impacted over 200 companies across agriculture, manufacturing, and construction.
